French Toast

Description:

Oh yummy, your favorite French Toast!

Ingredients:

  • 4 Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • 6 Slices of White Bread
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • Half & Half or milk
  • Cinnamon (if desired)
  • Your favorite syrup

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, vanilla, half/milk and vanilla to tase in a large glass bowl
  2. Preheat large frypan on medium and melt 1/8th stick butter
  3. Dip (separately) 2 slices of bread till soaked, then place in frying pan
  4. Cook 7.5 minutes, Flip slices, Cook additional 7.5 minutes
  5. Set aside cooked slices, more butter in pan, repeat steps 3-4
  6. Enjoy with sprinkled cinnamon / maple syrup to taste
