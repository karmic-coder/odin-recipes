Oatmeal

Description:

Oh yummy, your favorite organic rolled-oats oatmeal!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Organic Rolled Oats
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Salt or Sugar (to taste)
  • 1/4 Stick Butter

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Bring water, salt/sugar, milk/half n half to start boil on high heat approx 2.5 minutes
  2. Reduce heat to medium, Add rolled oats, stir in
  3. Cook for 6.5 minutes
  4. Reduce heat down to 3, and stir to desired consistency
