Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description:

Oh yummy, your favorite slow cooker pulled pork!

Ingredients:

  • Boneless pork tenderloin roast ~2lbs
  • 3 medium yellow onions

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Slice onions and evenly spead them as a bed across bottom of slow cooker
  2. Place tenderloin in slow cooker atop onions
  3. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder
  4. Cover tenderloin with poured over Sweet Baby Ray's Barbeque Sauce
  5. Cover and cook in slow cooker for 3 hours on high
  6. Carefully flip tenderloin
  7. Repeat steps 3-5
  8. Turn off slow cooker, allow to cool off for 30-60 minutes
  9. Finely shred pork with tongs, mix well
